## Proved Declarations (non-trivial)

- `terminator_is_choice` — §1.661: `one` is always choice in a regular category.
  Proof: any entire `R : BinRel 𝒞 A one` is automatically simple by terminal
  uniqueness (`term_uniq`), hence a map, so `R.colA` is iso and its inverse
  is the required section.

- `coprod_choice_to_one_one_choice` — §1.662 (1)→(2): immediate from `terminator_is_choice`.

## Key Blockers

### `amalgamation_lemma` (§1.651)
`EffectiveRegular` has no axioms beyond `RegularCategory` (placeholder only).
Need: every equivalence relation on B+C has a coequalizer (= quotient).
Fix: add `hasQuotient : ∀ {A}, EquivalenceRelation → ∃ (Q:𝒞)(x:A⟶Q), Cover x ∧ ...`
to `EffectiveRegular`.

### `pretopos_balanced` (§1.652)
Needs disjointness of coproducts (Freyd's positivity axiom, not just `HasBinaryCoproducts`).
The cokernel-pair of a monic m lives in B+B; monic+epic forces m to split.
Fix: add disjointness axiom to `PositivePreLogos` or `PreTopos`.

### `subobject_of_choice_is_choice`, `prod_choice_is_choice` (§1.66, §1.661)
Need: `Entire R → Entire (R ⊚ graph f)` for any morphism f.
Proof requires `compose_assoc` (§1.56, currently sorry).

### `one_one_choice_to_boolean` (§1.662)
Needs: slice pre-logos (§1.63), pushout in pre-topos, pullback of complemented
subobjects is complemented.  All depend on unavailable complement infrastructure.
