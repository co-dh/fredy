## Key gaps and how to close them

### `middle_two_interchange`
The `add` field of `HalfAdditiveCategory` is abstract — only `add_zero` and `zero_add` are
axiomatized, with no axiom connecting `add` to `prod_coprod_coincide`. The book's proof defines
TWO binary operations +_L (via coproduct codiagonal) and +_R (via product diagonal) and shows
they coincide, from which interchange follows from universality. The current class would need to
either derive `add` from `prod_coprod_coincide` rather than asserting it, or add an axiom
connecting the two. Since this fix touches the class definition (§1.591 structure), it is out of
scope for §1.58 alone.
