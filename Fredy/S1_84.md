## Blockers

- **Rel(E) as a category**: §1.842 and §1.847 (and ultimately §1.845/§1.846 uniqueness)
  require `Rel(E)` to have a `Cat` instance. `BinRel E A B` types relations between two fixed
  objects; the full `Rel(E)` category (objects = objects of E, morphisms = BinRel up to ≡) is
  not yet formalized. This is the biggest missing piece.

- **Pre-topos basis argument** (§1.843 well-powered): needs to show that in any pre-topos the
  generating set ℱ is a basis, i.e., every subobject is an equalizer (§1.52 + §1.62 machinery).
  Then `Sub(A) ↪ Π_{G∈ℱ} 𝒫(Hom(G,A))` gives smallness. Should be feasible with existing S1_52
  + S1_62 infrastructure.

- **Arbitrary-union composition** (§1.845/§1.846): composing a relation with an arbitrary
  supremum of subobjects; needs `LocallyComplete'` (§1.844, already in GrothendieckTopos) and
  the distributivity law (§1.844 "the composition of relations distributes with arbitrary unions").
