## Sharpened blockers for remaining sorries

### `quotientByKernel_exists` (§1.366) — faithful sorry
The quotient `𝒟 = 𝒞/K` must **glue K-connected objects**: `Q` cannot be identity on
objects, because the kernel clause demands `Q X = Q Y` for every K-map `f : X ⟶ Y`,
whereas `X ≠ Y` in general. So `|𝒟| = Quotient` of `|𝒞|` by `R X Y := ∃ f : X ⟶ Y, K.mem f`.

Two concrete blockers, in order:
1. **`R` is not provably an equivalence relation from the current `EquivalenceKernel`
   structure.** Reflexivity (`mem_id`) and symmetry (`isGroupoid`) are available, but
   **transitivity needs composition-closure** (`K.mem f → K.mem g → K.mem (f ≫ g)`),
   which is **not a field** of `EquivalenceKernel` (it has only `mem_id`, `isGroupoid`,
   `isPreorder`). A genuine kernel is closed under composition
   (`Q(fg)=Q f·Q g=1·1=1`); the abstract structure omits this. FIX OPTION: add a
   `mem_comp` field to `EquivalenceKernel` (book-faithful: a kernel is a subcategory),
   then `R` is an equivalence and `Quotient` of objects is well-defined.
2. Even with object-gluing, **morphisms = double cosets `KxK`** require `Quotient.lift`
   over hom-sets and **choice of object representatives** to state the `Cat` instance;
   the functor/embedding/fullness/representative-image/onto laws then each need the
   double-coset calculus. This is the genuinely deep part (hundreds of lines, AC for
   representatives).

### `equivalenceFunctor_factorization` (§1.361) — faithful sorry
Needs an inflation `I` of `𝒟` with `Φ : 𝒞 → I.objSet` a **cat-iso** (bijection on
objects) and `I.T (Φ X) = F X`. Since `Φ` is a bijection, `I.objSet ≅ 𝒞` and `I.T`
factors as `F` precomposed with that bijection — so `I.T` onto `|𝒟|` forces **`F` onto
on objects**. But an equivalence functor only has `HasRepresentativeImage` (every
`b : 𝒟` is *iso* to some `F a`), not literal surjectivity. Bridging iso-onto to onto
requires **choosing a representative object** per iso-class (AC). FIX: classical choice
of representatives, then `I.objSet := 𝒟` with `T := id` and `Φ b := chosen a`.
