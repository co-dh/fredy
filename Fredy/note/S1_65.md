## Blocker re-investigation (2026-06-16, second pass)

Attempted to close steps (i) and (iii); both confirmed genuinely blocked at the §1.543
effectiveness / Capitalization layer, with no callable fully-stated bridge under the
verbatim hypotheses.  Concrete findings (docstrings sharpened accordingly):

- **Step (i)**: located `pasting_lemma` (§1.62, `Fredy/S1_62.lean:495`, sorry-free) — the
  intersection of two monic subobjects `A₁, A₂ ↪ A` pushes out to their union `U = A₁ ∪ A₂`.
  This supplies the "bicartesian-of-monics" pushout half (the old docstring's claim that no
  such lemma exists was stale).  `pres_pushouts` then carries the span `(π₁,π₂)` to a pushout
  in ℬ — but onto `TU`, not the cospan apex `TA`.  Recovering the pullback of `(Tm,Tn)`
  needs the monic-pushout ⇒ pullback CONVERSE (union/intersection effectiveness, §1.543 =
  `amalgamation_lemma` leg-monicity, sorry in S1_64).  No `IsPullback`-from-pushout lemma
  exists.  Cannot close without a sorry.

- **Step (iii)**: two routes, both blocked.
  - Route A: `bicart_repr_preserves_covers` (§1.581, S1_58) reduces covers to
    `PreservesCoequalizers F`, which `pres_pushouts` does not give (coeq ≠ pushout of the
    parallel pair); and it needs `RegularCategory`/`HasCoequalizers` on both sides, absent here.
  - Route B (epi route, the cleaner idea): `pres_pushouts` ⇒ `T` preserves epis (epi ⟺
    cokernel-pair legs coincide; `T` of that pushout keeps them equal), then epic ⟹ cover via
    `cover_eq_epic_preTopos` (§1.64).  Blocked twice: (1) no `HasPushout f f` (cokernel pair
    `B +_A B`) constructible — `PreTopos` bundles no pushout/cocartesian existence and the
    verbatim signature lacks `[HasBinaryCoproducts]/[HasCoequalizers]`; (2) `cover_eq_epic_preTopos`
    needs `[HasReflTransClosure ℬ]` (not in scope) and internally calls `pretopos_balanced`,
    itself a §1.543 sorry.

Net: 0 of the 2 closed this pass; both honestly blocked.  Build remains green, no new sorries,
statements verbatim.

## Discharged previously

- **`coprod_is_pushout_of_init`** (private): A + B is the pushout of (0→A, 0→B). Proof
  directly from `HasBinaryCoproducts` + `HasCoterminator` axioms: any pushout cocone
  over the two initial maps automatically commutes by `init_uniq`, and `case` gives the
  mediating map.
- **Part (c) of `preTopos_functor_is_bicartesian_repr`**: coproduct preservation.
  Proof sketch: (1) use `coprod_is_pushout_of_init` to cast `coprod A B` as a pushout;
  (2) `PreservesInitial` makes any cocone over `(F∘init A, F∘init B)` automatically
  commute; (3) `PreservesPushouts` applied to that cocone gives the inverse map
  `inv : F(coprod A B) → coprod(FA,FB)`; (4) `fwd ≫ inv = id` by `case_uniq` on
  the inclusions; (5) `inv ≫ fwd = id` by pushout uniqueness applied to the self-cocone.
