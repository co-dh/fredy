## Sorries and Why

- **`inter_le_named`** (§1.943 pt1): requires the classify pullback axiom — that
  `InverseImage(χ_{A'}, true) ≅ A'`.  The `HasSubobjectClassifier` class in S1_9
  only asserts existence of `classify`, not its pullback square.  Blocked on
  extending that class or adding a separate axiom.

- **`inter_is_glb`** (§1.943 pt2): additionally needs the covariant Ω(−) functor
  to carry well-pointed epic families to monic families.  Full proof uses
  capitalization lemma (§1.54) and `inter_le_named` above.

- **`topos_has_strict_coterminator`** (§1.944): needs `inter_is_glb` for A=1, F=∅,
  then the strict-coterminator criterion from §1.331 (map-to-0 is iso iff no
  proper subobjects).  Blocked on the same classify gap.

- **`topos_is_regular`** (§1.945): images are ∩F for the image-of-f family;
  needs `inter_is_glb` + capitalization.  Also needs PreRegular⟹Regular path.

- **`topos_is_logos`** (§1.946): binary union A₁∪A₂ = ∩{B' | A₁⊆B' ∧ A₂⊆B'};
  right adjoint f## from exponential adjunction.  Depends on §1.943 and §1.945.

- **`topos_has_rtc`** (§1.947): needs `inter_is_glb` for the family F = F₁∩F₂
  of reflexive pre-closed relations; then ordinary relational argument closes.

- **`singletonMap`** (§1.94(10)): `curry(χ_{Δ_A})` — needs classify on the
  diagonal, which again requires the classify pullback axiom.

- **`singletonMap_entire`** (§1.94(10)): depends on singletonMap construction
  and classify pullback axiom.

- **`capital_is_solvable`** (§1.94(10)): needs capitalization lemma §1.54,
  `inter_is_glb`, and the strict-coterminator for the A*=0 case.

## Root cause of all sorries

`HasSubobjectClassifier` (S1_9) lacks the **pullback axiom**:
> m is the pullback of `true : 1 → Ω` along `classify m hm`.

Adding this to the class (or as a separate axiom file) would unblock the
entire chain §1.943 → §1.944 → §1.945 → §1.946 → §1.947 → §1.94(10).
