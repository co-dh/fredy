## Notes on triple_neg and double_neg_meet

The book states `¬¬¬x = ¬x` as an equation in the free Heyting algebra (ordering equality).
In the subobject setting, propositional equality `S = T` for subobjects requires more than
mutual `≤` (it would require subobject extensionality). We instead prove mutual `≤`
as `triple_neg_equiv` and `double_neg_meet_le/ge`, which capture the mathematical content.
If a propositional equality is needed, add a `Subobject.le_antisymm` axiom or use `Isomorphic`.
