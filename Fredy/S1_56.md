## Note — §1.561 (RS)° = S°R° is now constructive (no regularity)

The earlier "needs epi / regularity" claim was wrong.  `R⊚S` is `image ⟨π₁≫R.colA, π₂≫S.colB⟩`
over the pullback of `(R.colB, S.colA)`; `S°⊚R°` is `image ⟨π₁'≫S.colB, π₂'≫R.colA⟩` over the
pullback of `(S.colA, R.colB)`.  These two pullbacks are the SAME square with legs swapped, so the
canonical comparison `φ` satisfies `φ ≫ sp' = sp ≫ prodSwap` where `prodSwap = ⟨snd,fst⟩ : A×C → C×A`
is the self-inverse product-swap iso.  Hence the subobject `image sp' ≫ prodSwap⁻¹` of `A×C` allows
`sp` (witness `φ ≫ image.lift sp'`), and `image_min sp` produces the containment witness `k`.  The
reverse direction `comp_reciprocal_le` follows from `reciprocal_comp_le` applied to `S°, R°` plus
`reciprocal_invol` and `reciprocal_mono`.  Only `image_min` + product/pullback universal properties
are used — no cover-pullback stability, no Henkin-Lubkin.  Unblocks S1_77 and §1.562.

## Blocker — §1.56(10) `constant_image_subterminator`

Goal: `Mono (term (image x).dom)` from `Constant x` (∀ y y' : C→A, y≫x = y'≫x).  Need any
`u v : W → (image x).dom` equal.  `(image x).arr` is monic, so it suffices that
`u ≫ (image x).arr = v ≫ (image x).arr : W → B`.  But `Constant x` only equalizes maps that land
in `A` and are post-composed with `x = image.lift x ≫ (image x).arr`.  To transport `u, v` from
`(image x).dom` to `A` one must pull them back along the cover `image.lift x : A → (image x).dom`
and use that `image.lift x` is epic (a cover, `image_lift_cover`) PLUS that covers are stable under
pullback (regularity).  The plain `HasImages` context here lacks `RegularCategory`/cover-pullback
stability, so the elementary proof is unavailable.  FIX: either add a `[RegularCategory 𝒞]` instance
to the statement (Freyd states it for regular cats anyway) and use cover-pullback descent, or derive
it from the §1.563 Horn-sentence metatheorem once that is discharged.
