## 1. `omega_monic_endo_is_involution` (§1.919) — BLOCKED

Claim: a monic `g : Ω → Ω` satisfies `g ≫ g = id`.

- The old docstring blamed a missing `classify_unique`. That is wrong: `HasSubobjectClassifier`
  (Fredy/S1_9.lean:137) already provides `classify_unique`. Docstring corrected.
- The real gap is the **subobject algebra** Freyd's proof runs on:
  - the g-large-subobject relation (`A' ⊆ A` is g-large iff `g(A') = A`),
  - the equational theory of `omegaMeet` (∧) and `heytingDoubleArrow` (⇔). Both are only *defined*
    in this file (lines ~125–139) with **no proven laws**.
  - the bijection "maps `A → Ω` ↔ subobjects of `A`" used to move between `g²(A) = A` for all `A`
    and the single equation `g ≫ g = id`.
- `apply hm` (mono-cancel) reduces the goal only to `g ≫ g ≫ g = g` (`g³ = g`), still needing the
  same algebra. No tactic shortcut (`simp`, `aesop`) makes progress.
- Removable once §1.914's ∧/⇔ get their equational laws and the subobject↔characteristic-map
  correspondence over an arbitrary object is formalized.

## 2. `minimal_topos_has_terminator` (§1.91(10)) — BLOCKED + STATEMENT DEFECT

Claim: binary products + equalizers + subobject classifier + non-empty ⟹ a terminator exists.

- Faithful content = the `Λ(M_{B,B})` construction (constant idempotent on `[B]`, its equalizer with
  `id` is a terminator). That needs full power-object machinery (Λ/ε adjunction), beyond
  `HasSubobjectClassifier`.
- **Statement defect**: `HasSubobjectClassifier extends HasTerminal`, so the hypothesis
  `[HasSubobjectClassifier 𝒞]` already *contains* a terminator. `exact ⟨inferInstance⟩` type-checks
  (verified) but proves nothing — it re-exposes the assumed terminator, exactly what §1.91(10) says
  you need **not** assume. Closing it that way is rejected as vacuous, so it was left as a `sorry`.
- To close honestly, weaken the hypothesis to "binary products + equalizers + a power-object for 1
  (Ω with the classifier pullback property) but NO `HasTerminal`", then build `HasTerminal` as the
  equalizer of `id_{[B]}` and `Λ(M_{B,B})`. That rewrite + the power-object infra is the actual work.
