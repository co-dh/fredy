## Key Blocker: eps_thick axiom

The axiom `eps_thick (b : 𝒜) : Cat.id (powerObj b) ⊑ eps b / eps b` in `PowerAllegory` is
**vacuously true** in any division allegory by `one_le_div_self`. The real thickness condition
from §2.41 is: "for every R, there exists a map f such that f ≫ ∋ = R" (i.e., ∋ is "thick" in
the sense that R/∋ is entire for all R). This means `∀ R, Entire (A R)`.

Consequence: `A_is_map` (entire part) and `A_eps_eq` (lower bound) are unprovable from the
current axioms. `A_unique`, `simple_le_A_eps`, `singletonMap_monic` are proved without entireness.
