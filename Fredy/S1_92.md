## Direct-image power map on GENUINE power objects (this pass)

The prior assessment correctly noted that the **`exp A Ω`** version of `powerMapCov` cannot be defined
from the bare subobject-classifier `Topos` (`exp A Ω` carries no membership relation `∈_A`).  The honest
route is to work on the **genuine power object** `powerObj A` that S1_9 already packages as
`HasPowerObject A` (carrier `powerObj`, universal relation `mem : BinRel (powerObj A) A`, classifier
`powerClassify`).  Freyd's topos has `P(A) = Ω^A` for every `A`; the repo's minimal `Topos` just does not
bundle it, so — exactly as every other power-object result in the repo does — we take
`[HasPowerObject A] [HasPowerObject B] [HasImages 𝒞]` as **explicit, load-bearing** hypotheses.

- **`directImageRel f := ∈_A ⊚ graph f`** (NEW, axiom-free) — the §1.56 composite relation
  `BinRel (powerObj A) B`.  Unfolding `⊚`, its source is the image of the span
  `⟨mem.colA, mem.colB ≫ f⟩`, i.e. Freyd's existential image `{(P,b) | ∃a, (P,a)∈∈_A ∧ f a = b}`.
- **`powerMapCovP f := Λ(∈_A ⊚ graph f)`** (NEW, faithful) — the covariant direct-image power map
  `f" : powerObj A → powerObj B`, classified by the universality of `∈_A`.
- **`powerMapCovP_id` (NEW, PROVEN)** — the §1.96 identity **`f"f = 1` at `f = 1`**:
  `powerMapCovP 1_A = 1_{[A]}`.  Proof = universality uniqueness (`HasPowerObject.is_universal.classify_unique`):
  both `1_{[A]}` and `Λ(∈_A ⊚ graph 1_A)` classify the SAME relation, because
  `∈_A ⊚ graph 1_A ≅ ∈_A ≅ relPullback 1_{[A]} ∈_A` — the two new relation-isos
  `compose_graph_id` and `relPullback_id`, chained through `RelHom_trans`.  `#print axioms`:
  `propext, Classical.choice` (choice from `powerClassify`'s `∃`-elimination, as in
  `exponentials_of_all_baseable`), **NO `sorryAx`**.
- **Axiom hygiene fix** — `HasExponentials extends HasBinaryProducts` and `topos_has_exponentials` is a
  `sorry`, so a bare `HasBinaryProducts 𝒞` goal could route through it and contaminate every product/relation
  term with `sorryAx`.  Confirmed empirically (`R ⊚ graph 1` jumps from axiom-free to `sorryAx` once S1_92 is
  imported).  Fixed by `attribute [local instance 10000] Topos.toHasBinaryProducts` in the direct-image
  section (plus deprioritising `topos_has_exponentials` to 50).  With it, `directImageRel`/`powerMapCovP_id`
  are `sorryAx`-free.
