the per-`A` equalizer-of-transposes construction; the in-file "FAITHFUL SORRY" comment is stale text, the
proof body has no `sorry`). With that unblock, two of the three §1.92 load-bearing steps now exist here:

  pullbacks (the latter from `HasSubobjectClassifier`'s `HasPullbacks` base), and §1.434
  `products_pullbacks_implies_equalizers` builds eq(f,g) as the pullback of `⟨1,f⟩,⟨1,g⟩ : A ⇉ A×B`.
  `lean_verify`: **no axioms**.

  bridge `(∀ B : 𝒞, Baseable B) → HasExponentials 𝒞`. The representing object `E` and counit `ev` of
  `Baseable B` at stage `A` ARE `exp_obj A B` and `eval_map`; the representing map is `curry`; the β/η
  laws (`curry_eval`, `curry_unique`) are exactly the existence/uniqueness clauses of `Baseable`. Pins
  `toHasBinaryProducts := Topos.toHasBinaryProducts` to avoid the product diamond. `lean_verify`: only
  `Classical.choice` (unavoidable — `Baseable` is a bare `∃ E ev`, giving no canonical representing
  object), **no `sorryAx`**.

  graph map `ι = curry(γ)`, `γ = ⟨ eval(a,f), b ⟩ ≫ classify(diag B)` on `(A×B)×B^A` (was previously
  `⟨sorry, sorry⟩` with no map). The remaining single `sorry` is `Mono ι`: by `curry_precomp` + `curry_inj`

## Remaining blockers (all faithful sorries on real book statements)

1. **`topos_has_exponentials`** — reduced this pass to the SINGLE residual `∀ B, Baseable B` (fed to
   `exponentials_of_all_baseable`). The only missing step is now (a):
   - (a) **Every power object `[B]` is baseable**, i.e. representability `[B]^A ≅ [A×B]`. Needs a power
     object `[B] = HasPowerObject.powerObj` for EVERY `B` plus the Λ/∈ classify-bijection at product level.
     This repo's `Topos` is the *minimal subobject-classifier* presentation: it bundles only `Ω = [1]`,
     NOT `HasPowerObject C` for general `C`, and there is no construction of general power objects from the
     bare classifier anywhere in the repo (every power-object result, e.g. S1_91
     `minimal_topos_has_terminator`, *assumes* `[∀ C, HasPowerObject C]`). Without `[B]`, neither the
     singleton-equalizer presentation of `B` nor the representability iso can be formed, so "every object
     baseable" cannot be supplied.
   - (b) topos equalizers — **now DONE** (`topos_has_equalizers`).
   - (c) baseable-equalizer closure — **now DONE** (`baseable_equalizer_is_baseable`, S1_85, proved).

   **`sorry` shape note:** the instance is kept as an opaque `by sorry` (NOT
   `exponentials_of_all_baseable (fun _ => sorry)`) purely so it retains a computable IR stub — downstream
   files `S1_94 powObj` / `S1_95` build *computable* definitions on `exp`, and routing through the
   `Classical.choice`-based bridge would force them `noncomputable` (out of scope for this S1_92-only edit).
   The genuine assembly lives, fully proved, in `exponentials_of_all_baseable`.
2. **`powerMapCov`** — existential direct image [f] : [A] → [B].  **RE-ASSESSED** against the new S1_91
   classifier bijection (`classify_surjective` + `classify_unique`): still NOT definable, for a *sharper*
   reason than "image not packaged".  Two universal properties are needed and both are absent for `exp A Ω`:
   - (1) S1_91's bijection is `Sub(A) ≅ Hom(A, Ω=[1])`.  `[f]` needs the one-transpose-higher
     `Sub(A×X) ≅ Hom(X, Ω^A)` — the universal MEMBERSHIP relation `∈_A ⊆ Ω^A × A` and its `Λ`,
     i.e. `HasPowerObject A` (S1_9 `mem`/`classifyRel`), which the Ω-classifier does not lift to.
   - (2) Even with `HasPowerObject A`, its carrier `powerObj A ≇ exp A Ω` (no iso available), and
     `Topos 𝒞` does NOT bundle `∀ C, HasPowerObject C` (every repo power-object result takes it as an
     explicit `[∀ C, HasPowerObject C]` hyp).  The output type is forced to `exp A Ω`, which only carries
     curry/eval from `HasExponentials`, itself opaque (`topos_has_exponentials` is `sorry`, §1.543).

   §1.56's image factorization (`HasImages`) IS available and would supply the ∃-image once a membership
   relation existed to push along `f`; the gap is precisely the missing power-object structure on `exp A Ω`,
   not the image.  A `curry(<anything>)` + sorry'd `f"f=1` would be a hypotheses-unused fake (forbidden), so
   it stays a genuine, now-sharply-pinned `sorry`.
3. **`singletonMapCat_natural`** — f(Δ₁)=Δf, stated against `powerMapCov f`; the equation is precisely
   [f]'s defining property, not independently provable until [f] is defined.
4. **`expSubobj` Mono** — see above; residual is the single-valuedness of the graph predicate (faithfulness
   of `classify(diag B)`), the same mechanism as `singletonMapCat_monic` one transpose higher.
5. **`subTerminal_heyting`** — genuine Heyting adjunction `Z∧U≤V ↔ Z≤(U⇒V)` (not a tautology). Both
   directions reduce to curry/eval (β/η) of `Ω^U`, whose evaluation rests on `topos_has_exponentials`
