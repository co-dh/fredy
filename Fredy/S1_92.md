## Discharged this pass

- **`curry_precomp`** — reusable naturality lemma used by the monic proof.
- **`omegaPowContra.map_comp`** — full contravariant-functoriality of Ω^(−). Reduces RHS `Ω^g ≫ Ω^f`
  through `curry_unique_eq`, expands the inner `curry` via `curry_eval_eq`, and pushes the reindexing
  `pair (fst≫·) snd` through `prodMap` coordinatewise using `pair_uniq`.
- **`singletonMapCat_monic`** — Δ₁ is monic (the book's "as a consequence, Δ₁ is monic"). Built three
  helpers: the graph monic `graphMono h = ⟨h,1⟩ : X'↪B×X'` (monic via retraction `snd`), the identity
  `graphMono h ≫ (B×h) = h ≫ diag B`, and the key lemma `graph_classifies`: `B×h ≫ χ_Δ` is the
  characteristic map of `graphMono h`, with the graph square a pullback (obtained by pasting the diagonal
  classifier pullback with the pullback of the diagonal along `B×h`, via `classify_pullback`). Then if
  `h ≫ Δ₁ = k ≫ Δ₁`, `curry_precomp` + `curry_inj` give equal char maps, so `graphMono k` is a cone over
  `graphMono h`'s pullback; the lift `u` satisfies `u ≫ graphMono h = graphMono k`, hence (project `snd`)
  `u = 1` and (project `fst`) `h = k`.
- **`heytingImpl`** (this pass, new discharge) — U⇒V implemented as:
  `curry(snd ≫ V) ≫ omegaPowContra.map U ≫ prodOneLeftInv(Ω^^one) ≫ eval_exp one Ω`.
  - `curry(snd ≫ V) : one → Ω^^Ω` names V as the "constant" Ω-valued function via snd : prod Ω one → one.
  - `omegaPowContra.map U : Ω^^Ω → Ω^^one` is the contravariant Ω^U action (already proved in §1.922).
  - `prodOneLeftInv(Ω^^one) ≫ eval_exp one Ω : Ω^^one → Ω` is the left-unit iso Ω^1 ≅ Ω,
    available from S1_42 (newly imported). The blocker comment was wrong: `prod_one_iso_left` and
    `prodOneLeftInv` ARE in S1_42; adding `import Fredy.S1_42` unblocked this.

## Remaining blockers (all faithful sorries on real book statements)

1. **`topos_has_exponentials`** — book proof (§1.92): "power objects are baseable; B is an equalizer of
   χ and [B]→1→Ω; so B is baseable [§1.859]".

   **Re-checked this pass (S1_85 synced).** The §1.859 lemma `baseable_inclusion_preserves_equalizers`
   that was previously a bare `sorry` is now PROVED — but only in a WEAK, near-tautological form: it
   assumes `[HasEqualizers 𝒜]` plus a fully-supplied 𝔹-equalizer cone+lift and just returns
   `HasEqualizers.eq`. It does NOT prove the substantive CLOSURE Freyd uses — "the equalizer of two
   baseable objects is itself baseable". That closure is still absent from the repo.

   So the blocker is unchanged in effect but now SHARPENED to a triad, none available from this repo's
   `Topos` (= subobject-classifier presentation only):
   - (a) **Power-object representability** `[B]^A ≅ [A×B]` — needs `HasPowerObject C` for every C and the
     Λ/∈ classify-bijection at product level. No instance `Topos 𝒞 → ∀ C, HasPowerObject C` exists (the
     §1.912 equivalence is itself a `sorry` in S1_91); `Topos` exposes only Ω = [1].
   - (b) **Topos equalizers** — no `Topos 𝒞 → HasEqualizers 𝒞` instance.
   - (c) **Baseable-equalizer closure** — the genuinely missing §1.859 content (see above).
   Until all three exist, the eval/curry data for a general `B^A` cannot be assembled.
2. **`powerMapCov`** — the existential direct image [f] : [A] → [B]. Needs §1.56 regular image
   factorization assembled into a morphism `exp A Ω → exp B Ω` (the ∃_f action on the universal relation).
   §1.56's `image` exists but is not packaged as a power-object morphism. **Why it cannot even be
   *defined* honestly here:** the direct image needs the membership/Λ universal property of the power
   object `Ω^A = exp A Ω`, but `exp A Ω` is opaque (its `curry`/`eval` are the junk dictionary of the
   `sorry`'d `topos_has_exponentials`), and no `HasPowerObject A` exists for general `A`. Writing
   `powerMapCov := curry(<anything>)` and leaving `f"f=1` as a sorry would be a hypotheses-unused fake
   (forbidden), so it stays a genuine `sorry`.

   **Downstream reduction (this pass, in S1_95 — what `powerMapCov` now unblocks, made precise):** the
   keystone consumer `omega_is_internally_injective` (§1.961) is now reduced to *exactly* this sorry by two
   sorry-free S1_95 lemmas: `cover_of_section` (a split epi is a cover, axiom-free) and
   `expMap_omega_eq_omegaPow` (`expMap Ω f = omegaPowContra.map f` by `rfl`, reusing the PROVED §1.922
   Ω^(−) functoriality). Once `powerMapCov f` and its identity `expMap Ω f ≫ powerMapCov f = id` (from
   `f"f=1`) land, §1.961 closes as `cover_of_section (expMap Ω f) (powerMapCov f) <identity>`.
3. **`singletonMapCat_natural`** — f(Δ₁)=Δf. Stated against `powerMapCov f`, which is unfilled; the
   equation is precisely [f]'s defining property, so it is not independently provable until [f] is defined.
4. **`expSubobj`** — `exp A B` is opaque because `topos_has_exponentials` is sorry; no concrete monic

5. **`subTerminal_heyting`** — INTEGRITY FIX (this pass). The prior statement `∃ W, W = heytingImpl U V`
   was a tautology (proved by `⟨_, rfl⟩`, asserting nothing). Restated as the genuine Heyting adjunction

   (blocker 1). Imported `Fredy.S1_91` to reuse `omegaMeet` (DRY).
