  `curry_eval_eq`, `prodMap`, `eval_exp`, and `pair_fst_snd`. Requires pulling
  apart `pair(f,g) ≫ prodMap ≫ eval` through associativity and curry adjunction.
  Proof exists in principle but needs significant tactic work.

- **primRec**: Definition requires building `k : A×N → A×N×B` via iteratePair,
  then projecting. Needs `pair`/`fst`/`snd` combinatorics for A×N×B.

- **nno_is_coproduct**: The inverse of `[0,s]` is built by NNO iteration
  sending `0 ↦ inl(*)` and `n ↦ inr(n-1)` — but encoding this requires the
  exponential adjunction or a direct iteration argument.

- **nno_peano_property**: Requires §1.986 infrastructure (reflexive-transitive

- **ac_iff_iac_and_projective_one**: Needs the pullback-along-f argument from §1.974

take g = zero≫f. Uniqueness: zero∘(term N) = id₁ by terminal uniqueness.
