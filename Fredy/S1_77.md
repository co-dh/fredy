## Summary of sorries

3 sorry sites, all with faithful statements:

1. `equivClos_from_symm_transRefClos` (§1.775) — symmetry of transitive-reflexive closure and
   minimality over the join of R, R°, 1. Both require formalizing the join structure (relUnion
   from S1_60) as lying inside the EquivClos candidate; the join axioms are not yet connected
   to the EquivClos minimality witness.

2. `quotClos_is_transitive` (§1.787) — two sorries inside:
   - `h_stable_RR`: R ⊚ (R/R) ⊑ R/R — provable only once R ⊚ R ⊑ R (uses `R` being in `R/R`)
     or with the logos right-adjoint (§1.784).
   - `h_RR_le_bar`: R/R ⊑ R̄ — needs the logos characterization that R̄ ⊑ R/R iff R̄ ⊚ R ⊑ R
     (which uses R̄ maximality); this is essentially the §1.787 main theorem itself.
   Both blockers are resolved in a full logos (§1.784 gives R/S existence; the right adjoint
   closes the fixedpoint argument). Pending §1.784 formalization from S1_70.
